The $0 to Wealth Roadmap

A step-by-step guide to building your financial empire from scratch.

Step 1: Mindset & Education ($0)

Before you invest money, invest time. Complete the Quests in TeenTycoon to understand compound interest, inflation, and risk.

NerdWallet: How to Invest in Stocks

Step 2: The Safety Net ($100 - $1,000)

Open a High Yield Savings Account (HYSA). Build an emergency fund so you never have to sell your investments to cover an unexpected expense.

Bankrate: Best HYSA Rates

Step 3: First Investment ($10 - $500)

Open a custodial brokerage account. Buy your first fractional share of a broad market index fund (like an S&P 500 ETF) rather than individual stocks.

Investopedia: What is an ETF?

Step 4: The Automation Machine ($50/mo)

Set up an automatic monthly transfer from your checking account to your brokerage. Consistency beats timing the market.

Forbes: Dollar-Cost Averaging

Step 5: Let It Snowball (Years 5+)

Reinvest all dividends. Don't panic during market dips. Let compound interest do the heavy lifting for the next few decades.

Vanguard: Reinvesting Dividends
